Le Tamis

interaction researches, prototyping

Context

Part of an exhibition at the Vaulx-en-Velin Planetarium in 2015, in partnership with Erasme (Digital Living Lab of the Lyon Metropole). Led by an industrial designer and a prototype maker.

What we did

  • Researches about new ways to interact in the museography field
  • Plastic researches
  • Prototyping with Arduino and Processing

Le Tamis – « the Sieve » in English – is an interactive mediation device which proposes an hybrid experience, between physical and digital, based on digitally augmented physical phenomenons (pressure, gravity and acceleration). It is a research device which can be a medium for various instructive contents in museums.
