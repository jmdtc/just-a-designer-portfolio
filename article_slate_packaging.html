the Slate Packaging

branding, packaging, retail

Context

The Slate is a smart drawing pad developed by iskn that instantly digitizes your own creations made with your favorite drawing tools: any pens and pencils and any kind of paper â€“ even your notebook!

What I did

  • Benchmarking
  • Retail survey
  • Layout
  • Prototyping
  • Shooting
  • Collaboration with head of product, photographers, industrial designer

During my two years at iskn, I designed three different versions of the Slate and accessories packagings. I also designed every elements that enables the user to start with the Slate: rear side of the packaging, paper quickstart guide, included notebook, online quickstart guide, animations, etc.

Did you know?
The Slate is available at the MoMA Design Store in New York City and in over 300 famous retail locations in France and abroad (Fnac, Cultura, Darty, Harrods, etc).
