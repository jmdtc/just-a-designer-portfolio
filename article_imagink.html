Context

The Slate is a smart drawing pad developed by iskn that instantly digitizes your own creations made with your favorite drawing tools: any pens and pencils and any kind of paper â€“ even your notebook!

What I did

  • Benchmarking
  • User tests and researches
  • Ergonomics
  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Graphical assets
  • Collaborating with engineers and developers with Agile and Scrum methods

Imagink is the drawing application developed for the Slate on the iPad, iPhone, Android, Mac, and Windows. The app also offers a range of features to give life to your sketches like the video timelapse or the use of layers!

During my two years at iskn, I designed the interfaces of every Imagink versions from the very first one to the newest.
