the Slate Shake & the Slate Squad

social networks, logotype, motion design

Context

The Slate is a smart drawing pad developed by iskn that instantly digitizes your own creations made with your favorite drawing tools: any pens and pencils and any kind of paper – even your notebook!

Deliverables

  • Logotypes
  • Social network content
  • Animations
  • Print works

During my two years at iskn, I was responsible for visual branding and interface design. I built the visual brand identity and created its brandbook, I also took part of the iskn activities on the social networks. I designed two subidentities of the brand.

The Slate Squad is an ambassador program. I designed the logotype and some visuals elements for the website. The Slate Shake is a tutorial webshow about the Slate. I designed the logotype, the opening and closing animations and other layout elements.
