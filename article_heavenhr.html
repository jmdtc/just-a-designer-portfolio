HeavenHR

UX/UI design, webdesign, marketing

Context

I have worked for HeavenHR since the beginning of 2018 as a UI/UX and graphic designer. HeavenHR is a company that offers a unique HR software. Thanks to its product, companies can manage all their HR practices in one single online platform.

Responsibilities

  • Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off
  • Propose usability improvements
  • Wireframe and prototype to communicate interaction and ideas
  • Create communication mediums for digital and print channels

I work closely with our product manager and developers on improving the platform and creating new features. Since I am the only designer, I am responsible for every design steps, from creative brainstorming to prototyping and graphic execution. I also collaborate with the marketing team to improve our website and create communication mediums for the print and digital channels.
