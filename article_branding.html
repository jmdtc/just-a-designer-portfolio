iskn Branding

marketing, brandbook, print

Context

The Slate is a smart drawing pad developed by iskn that instantly digitizes your own creations made with your favorite drawing tools: any pens and pencils and any kind of paper – even your notebook!

Deliverables

  • Brandbook
  • Print works
  • Packaging and in-store ads
    •
  • Online brand content

During my two years at iskn, I was responsible for visual branding and interface design. Iskn was a very young startup when I integrated the team and the visual mediums weren’t consistent.

So I built step by step the visual brand identity, by designing print works (flyers, posters, postcards etc), packagings, in-store ads and other digital elements such as online advertising, illustrations or animations, in collaboration with the marketing team. Finally, I created the company brandbook.
